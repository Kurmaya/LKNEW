Akina

The Akina is a humanoid incubator designed for those parents who aren't physically able to bear children.This bot can fit almost everywhere, right from your home to your car. The Akina is a real paradise for the embryo. It's design replicates that of a human womb, to let your baby grow in perfect conditions.

The incubator maintains the optimum temperature, oxygen, hormones, nutritional intake for the foetus to ensure it is safe and grows into a perfectly healthy baby.

Bubblebot

Bubble bot is a powerful drone that you can keep by your side and quickly launch it to run on varied surfaces against gravity. It is capable of transitioning from ground to wall and perpendicular surfaces. The mechanism, materials and components used, makes this bot a cost effective product. It has a carbon fibre body making it light weight and rigid. It delivers a enough power to move across different surfaces and can be effortlessly piloted through the Internet. It can run autonomously and can also be controlled and driven manually when/if required. We predict the use of this bot for structural health monitoring, Infrastructure Inspection, photography, Asset Management, Millitary Purpose, Building Facade cleaning (glazing).

Antipode

A hypersonic business aircraft concept designed to fly from New York to London in 11 minutes. Rocket boosters will initially lift the aircraft from the ground to an altitude of 12,000 m (40,000 ft) and propel it to Mach 5, when scramjets will ignite accelerating the aircraft up to Mach 24. A nozzle in the aircraft's nose will apply NASA's Long Penetration Mode concept to scoop-up air to cool the aircraft.
